story

A revisited story of Romeo and Juliet taking place in the early 1990s where 2 Hip Hop breakdance bands are competing against each other.

Romeo and Juliet will try to live their love story despite the competition. This is an immersive and open performance where the public can walk around the actors. The performance takes all the space of the venue and the actors move around telling the story, using beatboxing and breakdancing.

R&J, A Street Romance 2019
